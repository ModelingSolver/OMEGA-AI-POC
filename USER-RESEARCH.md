## Methodology

### Goal
Understand specific cognitive needs, pain points, and tool expectations to inform the development of specialized OMEGA-AI modules.

### Ethics
- Participants informed of research purpose
- Data anonymized for publication
- No commercial use of data
- Participation voluntary

**Participant's Own Summary (prepared with AI assistance)**

## Emerging Patterns

### Shared Across Both Participants
- Desire for external cognitive support (both describe a "prosthesis" or "anchor")
- Difficulty with standard tools ‚Äî abandoned quickly or never adopted
- Awareness of their own profile ‚Äî both articulate and self-reflective
- Preference for tools that adapt to them, not tools that "normalize" them

### Divergence
| Dimension | Theo (ASD) | Alex (ADHD) |
|---|---|---|
| Primary pain | Social comprehension | Attention & executive function |
| Suffering level | Low-moderate (3/10) | High (10/10) |
| Identity stance | Accept self, navigate world | Wants to change, be "fixed" |
| Tool need | Verbalization partner | Calm & structure provider |
| Social profile | Functional, employed | Isolated, unemployed |

## Design Implications for OMEGA-AI

### Module: ASD Support
- **Social Context Decoder** ‚Äî help interpret ambiguous social situations
- **Verbalization Assistant** ‚Äî guided externalization of daily events and emotions
- **Communication Advisor** ‚Äî help formulate messages with appropriate tone
- **Emotion Mapper** ‚Äî help identify and name what others may be feeling

### Module: ADHD Support
- **Cognitive Calm Layer** ‚Äî reduce mental noise, provide structured grounding
- **Working Memory Extension** ‚Äî retain context and threads across session
- **Task Activation Support** ‚Äî micro-step initiation for procrastination paralysis
- **Attention Anchor** ‚Äî gentle refocusing without judgment

### Shared Architecture Requirement
The CT/MT/LT memory system with Heat Score is validated as essential:
- Continuity across sessions (critical for both profiles)
- No repetition required from user (reduces friction)
- Adaptive context = reduced cognitive load
